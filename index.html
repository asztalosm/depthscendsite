Depthscend

Depthscend is a dungeon crawler in an underwater setting. The player's party embarks on a journey to save the people in a recently sunken ship.
The game features 3 characters which the player can control by either manually moving them or giving them commands while controlling another character. The ship and the surrounding trenches are filled with artifacts that give the characters special powers. The dungeon consists of multiple floors which are randomly generated so no two runs are the same.
